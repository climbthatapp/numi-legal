Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Numi ("the App") handles information when you use it. Numi is developed and operated by Artur Tkachenko ("Developer", "we", "us").

1. Information We Process

The App processes the following categories of information:

  • Coin photos you capture or select to identify coins (obverse/reverse images).
  • Coin analysis results generated by the App (for example: coin metadata, valuation ranges, confidence).
  • Coin chat content you enter and AI responses within the in-app chat.
  • Basic support information if you contact us by email (for example, your email address and message).

2. How We Use Information

We process information only to provide core functionality:

  • Identify coins from images and return results in the App.
  • Enable coin-related chat assistance based on your saved coin data and messages.
  • Store your coin collection and chat history locally on your device.
  • Respond to support requests you send to us.

3. AI Processing and Third-Party Services

To provide coin identification and chat features, the App sends relevant content (for example, coin photos and chat text) to AI services through AIProxy/OpenAI endpoints configured by the Developer.

You should review those providers' terms and privacy disclosures as they may process request data according to their own policies.

4. Data Storage and Retention

  • On-device storage: Coin records, associated coin images, and chat messages are stored locally using Apple SwiftData.
  • Retention: Local data remains on your device until you delete it in the App or uninstall the App.
  • Remote processing: AI request content is transmitted for processing; retention on third-party systems is governed by those providers.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information and do not use your data for third-party advertising.

We only share data with service providers as needed to run core features (for example, AI processing providers).

6. Your Choices and Rights

  • You can stop sharing new photos or chat messages at any time by not using those features.
  • You can delete stored coin/chat data from the App and by uninstalling the App.
  • You can contact us to request access, correction, deletion, or export support where applicable law provides those rights.

7. International Transfers

Service providers used for AI processing may operate in countries outside your own. By using AI features, you understand that data may be processed in those countries.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can address the issue.

9. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date and may provide in-app notice.

11. Contact

For questions or privacy requests, contact: contact@climbthatapp.com
